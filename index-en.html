Our Company

  • Pacific Marble Group (PMG) is a one-stop stone solutions company.
     
  • PMG is the first stone subcontractor to become a listed company in Hong Kong (stock code 1592.hk). We have over 35 years of experience in this business field. Our main business scope is extravagant and landmark projects in Hong Kong, Macau, and PRC.
     
  • PMG is a comprehensive stone enterprise integrating stone quarry development, oversea procurement, shop drawing design, stone fabrication and installation. With years of operation and understanding of stones from all over the world we provide extensive and excellent services to all our clients.
